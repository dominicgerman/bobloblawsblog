About me

I am a web developer, digital content manager, and professional singer. I'm one half of Mount Prospect Web Design, a small business that designs and builds web solutions for other small businesses.

My primary skills are in frontend where I use JavaScript, CSS, React, and Svelte. I also know some Node.js, SQL, Docker, AWS, Linux, and PHP.

When I'm not programming, I like to sing in vocal ensembles around Chicago and beyond. In 2023, I won a GRAMMY award as a soloist on Born, released by The Crossing, singing the music of Edie Hill.

My wife and I enjoy working together, entertaining, and raising our Mini Australian Shepherd.
